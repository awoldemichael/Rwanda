"Contraceptive methods. Contraceptive methods are grouped into two categories: modern methods and
traditional methods. Modern methods include female sterilization, male sterilization, the pill, the IUD,
injectables, implants (such as Norplant), the female condom, the male condom, lactational amenorrhea
method (LAM), emergency contraception, the diaphragm, and foam/jelly. Traditional methods include
periodic abstinence, withdrawal, and any country-specific traditional methods. pp 5 - DHS report"
